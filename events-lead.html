Event Leads (Infor SCS)

Purpose

This guide describes how to manage and track event leads in Infor SCS.

Steps

  1. Access the Event Leads Module
    Go to Sales â†’ Event Leads.
  2. Create a New Lead
    Click New Lead and fill in lead details:
    • Customer Name
    • Event Type
    • Date and Venue
    • Contact Information
  3. Assign a Sales Representative
    From the drop-down list, select a sales rep responsible for the event.
  4. Save and Track
    Click Save to record the lead.
    The lead now appears in the Event Leads dashboard for tracking and follow-up.

Outcome

A new event lead is successfully created and assigned. Sales staff can monitor its progress and convert it to an active booking when finalized.
